Owl

is a tool for synthetic biologists that is designed to make it easy for them to generate datasheets. I was brought on to the project just a few days before the paper this tool is based on went to its reviewers. I worked to patch a number of bugs that had been logged by people on the team that pertained to stylings as well as functionality.

The bulk of my work was on the front end working with Javascript. Through a combination of Node.js and Cloudinary (an image hosting service) I was able to integrate the ability to upload images through the tool. All of the bugs were patched when the paper went to the reviewers.

owlcad.org

Nona

Research Foundation is an organization that is commited to the development of open source web applications and tools for the fields of synthetic biology and biodesign automation.

My role was in designing and building the site that would serve as the online home for this great organization. The site didn't require a server side language and was built simply with HTML/CSS/Javascript.

Eugene

is an ecosystem of languages tailored for rule-based design of synthetic biological systems, devices, parts, and DNA sequences.

I designed and built the site and also designed the logos for a variety of the tools. This site is lightweightt and didn't require a server side language and was built simply with HTML/CSS/Javascript.
